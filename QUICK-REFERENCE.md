## Core Systems at a Glance

### Weapons (5 Types)
1. **Swords** - Balanced, versatile
2. **Spears** - Reach, piercing
3. **Fans** - Speed, defense
4. **Umbrellas** - Tank, hybrid
5. **Rope Darts** - Range, mobility

### Skills
- **Martial Arts**: 12 total (weapon-dependent)
- **Mystic Skills**: 23 total (equip 8)
- **Inner Ways**: (Details TBD)

### Qigong Techniques
1. **Magic Hand** - Object manipulation
2. **Chi Grip** - Energy grip
3. **Cloud Step** - Aerial movement
4. **Lion's Roar** - Area attack/stun

### Careers (2 Available)
1. **Healer** - Support role
2. **Scholar** - Knowledge-based
