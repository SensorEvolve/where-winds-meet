Frequently Asked Questions

Is the app free?

Yes. Where Winds Meet Guide is completely free to download and use, with no ads, no in-app purchases, and no subscriptions.

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. All content is bundled with the app and works entirely offline. You can use it anywhere, even mid-dungeon without a signal.

What languages are supported?

The app is available in English, Traditional Chinese, Japanese, and Korean. The app will automatically detect your device language.

The content seems outdated. Will it be updated?

Yes. We actively update the app as new game content is released and documented. If you notice something that's missing or outdated, please open an issue and we'll prioritize it.

Can I contribute content?

Absolutely. The project is open source. Visit the GitHub repository to see how to get involved.

What data does the app collect?

None. See our Privacy Policy for the full details. The short version: no accounts, no tracking, no data ever leaves your device.
