# Where Winds Meet - Skill Theft (技能偷学)

## Overview

**Skill Theft** is a core feature in Where Winds Meet that allows players to **learn abilities by observing NPCs and creatures**, then completing a timing-based mini-game. This mechanic unlocks powerful abilities that enable progression and significantly expands the player's skill options.

**Importance**: Skill Theft is essential for:
- Unlocking unique Mystic Skills
- Learning rare abilities from animals and NPCs
- Discovering hidden skills not available through quests
- Mastering techniques that can make the difference between victory and defeat

**Golden Rule**: When a Skill Theft opportunity appears, don't ignore it! Take time to learn and practice the new skill.

---

## What Is Skill Theft?

While wandering the lands of **Qinghe**, **Kaifeng**, and beyond, players will encounter opportunities to learn skills through various methods:

**Traditional Learning Methods**:
- Skill Books
- Quest Rewards
- Trainers and Masters

**Skill Theft (Unique Method)**:
- Watch someone (NPC or creature) perform a skill from a distance
- Complete a timing-based mini-game
- Instantly learn the skill upon successful completion
- Practice the skill immediately after learning

## How to Unlock Skill Theft

### Unlock Quest: "Skill Theft: Unearned Lesson"

**Location**: Moonveil Peak (Front Mountain)

**Steps to Unlock**:

1. **Travel to Moonveil Peak**
   - Head or teleport to **Front Mountain**
   - Or use **Moonveil Peak Boundary Stone**

2. **Find Qui Yuehai NPC**
   - Locate the NPC at Moonveil Peak
   - Exhaust all dialogue options with him

3. **Start the Exploration Quest**
   - Quest Name: **"Skill Theft: Unearned Lesson"**
   - Quest Type: Exploration Quest
   - Quest Difficulty: Early game (Level 8+)

4. **Complete the Quest**
   - Follow quest objectives
   - Reward: **Skill Theft ability** unlocked permanently

**Requirements**:
- **Minimum Level**: Level 8
- **Location Access**: Moonveil Peak unlocked

**Reward**:
- **Skill Theft ability** (permanent)
- Ability to learn skills from NPCs and creatures throughout the game

## Skill Theft Mini-Game

### Overview

**Type**: Timing-based reflex challenge

**Objective**: Fill the **Comprehension Meter** at the bottom of the screen to 100% before time runs out

**Failure**: If time expires before comprehension is complete, you must restart the observation

---

### Mini-Game Mechanics

#### The Comprehension Meter

**Location**: Bottom of screen during observation

**Function**:
- Tracks your understanding of the skill
- Increases when you correctly time button presses
- Decreases when you make mistakes
- Must reach 100% to learn the skill

**Visual**: Progress bar showing current comprehension level

---

#### Golden Ring Opportunities (CORRECT)

**Appearance**: Golden/yellow ring appears around the skill practitioner

**Action**: Press the **Comprehend button** (bottom of screen) when you see the golden ring

**Result**:
- ✅ **Increases Comprehension** significantly
- Correct timing = larger comprehension boost
- Critical to successfully learning the skill

**Tip**: Watch the master's movements carefully and anticipate when golden rings will appear

---

#### Gray Circle Traps (WRONG)

**Appearance**: Gray circle appears instead of golden ring

**Action**: **DO NOT press Comprehend button** on gray circles

**Result**:
- ❌ **Decreases Comprehension** (penalty)
- Can set you back significantly
- May cause you to fail the timing challenge

**Strategy**: Wait for golden rings, ignore gray circles completely

---

#### Multiple Ring Sequences

**Special Moments**: Some sections have multiple opportunities in rapid succession

**Appearance**: Several golden rings appear in quick sequence

**Action**: Press Comprehend button for each golden ring

**Result**:
- ⭐ **Large Comprehension boost** for getting all rings
- Missing any ring = lost opportunity
- Getting all rings = massive progress toward completion

**Challenge**: Requires fast reflexes and concentration

---

### Tips for Success

1. **Watch Carefully**: Observe the master's movements to predict golden ring timing
2. **Don't Rush**: Wait for golden rings, don't spam the button
3. **Avoid Gray Circles**: Penalties can force a restart
4. **Focus on Sequences**: Multi-ring moments are key to fast completion
5. **Stay Calm**: The timer is usually generous if you don't make mistakes
6. **Practice Makes Perfect**: Failed attempts help you learn the timing

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I fail Skill Theft permanently?**
A: No, you can retry as many times as needed if you fail the mini-game

**Q: Do I need to re-learn skills on alts/new characters?**
A: Yes, Skill Theft must be done per character

**Q: Can Skill Theft opportunities be missed forever?**
A: Most can be done at any time, but check quest descriptions for time-sensitive ones

**Q: Is Skill Theft required for game completion?**
A: Some main quest skills (like Tai Chi) are required; others are optional but highly recommended

**Q: Can I Skill Theft from other players in PvP?**
A: No, Skill Theft only works on NPCs and creatures
