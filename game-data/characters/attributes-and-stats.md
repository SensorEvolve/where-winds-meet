# Where Winds Meet - Character Attributes & Stats

## Core Attributes

These fundamental attributes define character performance in combat and exploration.

### Combat Attributes

1. **Health (HP)**
   - Total hit points
   - Determines survival capacity
   - Can be increased through equipment, skills, and progression

2. **Chi (Internal Energy)**
   - Powers Qigong techniques and special abilities
   - Resource for mystic skills
   - Regenerates over time
   - Can be managed through meditation or items

3. **Stamina**
   - Used for dodging, sprinting, and physical actions
   - Depletes during intense activity
   - Regenerates when resting
   - Important for combat mobility

### Additional Combat Stats (Assumed)

- **Attack Power**: Physical damage output
- **Defense**: Damage reduction
- **Critical Hit Chance**: Chance for enhanced damage
- **Critical Hit Damage**: Multiplier for critical hits
- **Attack Speed**: Rate of attack execution
- **Movement Speed**: Character mobility
- **Evasion**: Chance to dodge attacks
- **Accuracy**: Hit chance against enemies
