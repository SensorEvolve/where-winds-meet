# Where Winds Meet - Builds & Playstyles

## Build System Overview

**Class System**: Where Winds Meet does NOT have traditional classes

**Flexibility**: Most combat skills are tied to weapons, allowing for flexible build creation

**Customization**: Mix and match weapons, martial arts, and mystic skills

---

## Known Build Archetypes

### 1. High Mobility Burst DPS

**Focus**: Speed and burst damage

**Characteristics**:
- Fast movement abilities
- High single-target damage in short windows
- Quick engagements
- Hit-and-run tactics

**Recommended**:
- Light weapons (possibly fans, rope darts)
- Mobility mystic skills (Cloud Step likely)
- High attack power, critical hit stats

---

### 2. Bleed-Focused Damage Dealer

**Focus**: Damage over time through bleeding effects

**Characteristics**:
- Apply bleed status to enemies
- Sustained damage output
- DOT management
- Multiple target capability

**Recommended**:
- Weapons with bleed effects (possibly swords, spears)
- Skills that enhance bleed duration/damage
- Stats: Attack power, bleed chance

---

### 3. Tank/Shield Build

**Focus**: Defense and enemy control

**Characteristics**:
- High survivability
- Damage reduction abilities
- Shield mechanics
- Enemy taunting capability
- Group protection (co-op/MMO)

**Recommended**:
- Heavy weapons or defensive weapons (umbrellas?)
- Defense-oriented martial arts
- Stats: Health, Defense, Damage Reduction

---

### 4. Ranged DPS with Airborne Combos

**Focus**: Distance attacks and aerial combat

**Characteristics**:
- Ranged attack capability
- Airborne combo potential
- Positioning advantages
- Mobility in air

**Recommended**:
- Ranged weapons (rope darts, possibly fans)
- Aerial mystic skills
- Stats: Attack power, mobility

---

### 5. Rapid Melee Assault

**Focus**: Fast, consecutive melee attacks

**Characteristics**:
- High attack speed
- Close-quarters combat
- Combo chains
- Sustained DPS

**Recommended**:
- Fast melee weapons (swords, rope darts)
- Attack speed buffs
- Stats: Attack speed, stamina management

---

## Build Components

### Weapon Selection
- Choose 1-2 primary weapons
- Weapon determines available martial arts
- Switch weapons mid-combat

### Martial Arts (Up to 12)
- Weapon-dependent skills
- Offensive and defensive options
- Build around weapon synergies

### Mystic Skills (Choose 8 from 23)
- Independent of weapons
- Combat and utility functions
- Critical for build identity

### Inner Ways
- Passive enhancements
- Long-term character development
- Details TBD

### Equipment
- Armor sets with bonuses
- Accessories for stat optimization
- Build-specific gear

## Hybrid Builds

**Flexibility**: The system appears to support hybrid approaches

**Examples**:
- Tank/Healer (Career + Tank build)
- Burst DPS/Mobility (High damage + escape)
- Bleed/Ranged (DOT from distance)
