# Where Winds Meet - Game Overview

## Basic Information

- **Title**: Where Winds Meet
- **Developer**: Everstone Studios
- **Publisher**: NetEase
- **Genre**: Open World Wuxia Action RPG
- **Business Model**: Free-to-play with cosmetic microtransactions and season passes
- **Global Release Date**: November 14, 2025
- **Platforms**: PC (Steam, Epic Games Store, Microsoft Store), PlayStation 5, Mobile

## Launch Success

- **First Weekend**: Over 2 million players
- **Peak Concurrent (Steam)**: 190,000 players
- **Steam Reviews**: Very Positive

## Setting & Theme

**Time Period**: 10th-century China during the Five Dynasties and Ten Kingdoms era

**Genre**: Wuxia (Chinese martial arts fantasy)

**World**: Large open-world map spanning 20 distinct regions

## Story & Premise

Players take on the role of a young sword master, embarking on a journey to:
- Uncover forgotten truths
- Discover the mysteries of their own identity
- Navigate the turbulent historical period of divided China

## Game Scope

- **Solo Campaign**: 100-150+ hours of narrative-driven gameplay
- **Co-op Mode**: Seamless 4-player cooperative gameplay
- **MMO Mode**: Shared online world with thousands of players
- **Cross-play**: Full cross-play and cross-progression support across all platforms

## Key Features

1. **Fluid Wuxia Combat**: Martial arts-inspired combat with swordplay, counters, and parries
2. **Qigong Abilities**: Special techniques like Magic Hand, Chi Grip, Cloud Step, Lion's Roar
3. **Weapon Variety**: Multiple weapon types with unique playstyles
4. **Extensive Skill System**: 12 martial arts and 23 mystic arts
5. **Open World Exploration**: 20 distinct regions to explore
6. **Flexible Gameplay**: Choose between solo, co-op, or MMO experiences

## Console Exclusivity

PlayStation 5 has a timed console exclusive for minimum 6 months following launch (potential Xbox release expected mid-2026).
