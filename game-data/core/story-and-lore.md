# Where Winds Meet - Story & Lore

## Historical Setting

### Five Dynasties and Ten Kingdoms Era (907-979 CE)

**Historical Context**:
- Period of political upheaval in China
- Followed the fall of the Tang Dynasty
- Multiple competing kingdoms and dynasties
- Era of martial prowess and wandering warriors

## Player Character

**Role**: Young sword master

**Journey Themes**:
- Identity discovery
- Uncovering forgotten truths
- Personal growth through martial arts mastery
- Navigation of a divided, turbulent China

## World Regions

The game features **20 distinct regions** across 10th-century China.

### Known Characteristics:
- Diverse landscapes reflecting historical Chinese geography
- Regional factions and conflicts
- Historical and fantastical elements blended

## Wuxia Elements

**Wuxia Genre Characteristics**:
- Martial arts mastery
- Qi/Chi manipulation (internal energy)
- Honor and code of warriors
- Wandering heroes (jianghu culture)
- Supernatural martial abilities
- Master-student relationships
- Sect rivalries
