# Where Winds Meet - Additional Game Mechanics

## Crime & Theft System

### Heavenly Snatch (天机窃取)

**Type**: Theft ability

**Mechanics**:
- Can steal from NPCs or locations
- Success/failure system
- Failed attempts can arouse suspicion
- Requires stealth and timing

**Related Exploration Skills**:
- **Shadow Theft**: Less likely to arouse suspicion when theft fails (400 Total Aspiration, Mindset 50, Elegance 30)
- **Thousand Hands**: Increases theft success rate (600 Total Aspiration, Coordination 75, Perception 50)

---

### Forcible Demanding

**Type**: Intimidation/extortion

**Mechanics**:
- Force NPCs to give you items
- Classified as a crime
- Crime probability system exists
- May have reputation/faction consequences

**Related Exploration Skills**:
- **Strength of Conviction**: Reduces crime probability by 50% when forcibly demanding (400 Total Aspiration, Erudition 50, Elegance 30)

## Disease System

### Disease Mechanics

**How You Get Sick**:
- High falls (falling damage can cause disease)
- Other sources TBD

**Disease Progression**:
- Disease can "evolve" (get worse over time)
- Can reach "Spellbound state" (critical condition)
- Deals damage over time
- Particularly dangerous in Healer mode gameplay

**Disease Management**:
- **Self-Healing**: Automatic chance to cure yourself
- **Healer Career**: Likely can cure diseases
- **Treatment Items**: Assumed to exist

---

### Related Exploration Skills

**Disease Prevention**:
- **Body of Diamond**: Lowers chance of contracting disease from high falls (400 Total Aspiration, Musicality 50, Perception 30)

**Disease Resistance**:
- **Inner Peace**: Chance to resist disease when approaching Spellbound state (400 Total Aspiration, Mindset 50, Constitution 30)

**Disease Recovery**:
- **Mystic Recovery**: Increases chance of Self-Healing during disease evolution (600 Total Aspiration, Musicality 75, Constitution 50)

**Disease Mitigation**:
- **Healer's Edge**: Reduces disease damage by 30% in first round of open-world Healer mode (600 Total Aspiration, Memory 75, Erudition 50)

---

### Spellbound State

**Description**: Critical disease condition

**Effects**:
- Severe state of illness
- Can be resisted with Inner Peace skill
- Likely has serious gameplay consequences

**Recovery**:
- Inner Peace skill provides resistance
- Other recovery methods TBD
