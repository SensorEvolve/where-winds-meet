# Where Winds Meet - Mounts

## Mounts System Overview

**Purpose**: Mounts provide increased travel speed and various helpful perks for traversing the Jianghu

**Basic Function**: Faster travel across the game world

**Special Functions**: Rare and special mounts offer unique bonuses for exploration, collection, and traversal

---

## Mount Categories

### Cash Shop Mounts

The best mounts can be purchased in the cash shop, featuring special abilities that grant faster travel and additional functions.

**Special Features**:
- Unique abilities and skills
- Enhanced traversal bonuses
- Additional utility functions
- Exclusive appearance options

---

### Standard Mounts

Standard mounts can be obtained through regular gameplay.

**Acquisition Methods**:
- **Horse Theft** - Steal horses from enemies
- **Stallion Ranches** - Capture and tame wild horses roaming freely at ranches
- **Exploration Rewards** - Unlock through exploration activities
- **Events** - Obtain during special in-game events

**Appearance Levels**: Some mounts have multiple appearance levels that evolve with progression

---

## Mount Skills

Each mount may possess its own skills, which affect different aspects of gameplay:

**Skill Categories**:
- **Traversal Skills** - Movement speed and mobility enhancements
- **Collection Skills** - Aid in gathering resources
- **Exploration Skills** - Help with discovering locations and treasures
- **Utility Skills** - Additional functions like carrying passengers or transforming

## Mount Acquisition Guide

### Cash Shop Mounts

**How to Obtain**:
1. Access in-game Cash Shop
2. Browse mount selection
3. Purchase with premium currency

**Advantages**:
- Unique appearance
- Exclusive skills
- Enhanced utility functions

---

### Standard Mounts

#### Horse Theft

**Method**: Steal horses from enemies during combat or infiltration

**Steps**:
1. Locate enemy with mount
2. Defeat or stealth approach
3. Mount the horse to claim it

---

#### Stallion Ranches

**Method**: Capture and tame wild horses

**Steps**:
1. Visit Stallion Ranches across the map
2. Find untamed steeds roaming freely
3. Approach and tame the wild horse
4. Add to your mount collection

**Best For**: Players who prefer earning mounts through gameplay

---

#### Exploration Rewards

**Method**: Unlock through exploration activities

**Activities**:
- Complete exploration milestones
- Discover hidden locations
- Achieve exploration rank requirements

---

#### Events

**Method**: Participate in special in-game events

**Characteristics**:
- Time-limited availability
- Unique event-exclusive mounts
- May have special appearance variants
