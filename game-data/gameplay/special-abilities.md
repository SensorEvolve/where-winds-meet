# Where Winds Meet - Special Abilities & Mechanics

## Touch of Death (暗杀 - Assassination)

### Overview
**Type**: Stealth kill mechanic

**Function**: Execute enemies or deal massive damage without being detected

**Requirements**:
- Enemy must be unaware (use Veil of Stillness)
- Approach from behind or when enemy is blinded

### Related Mystic Skills

**Veil of Stillness** (敛息):
- Reduces enemy detection
- Makes it easier to approach for Touch of Death
- Cannot be used in combat
- Must approach from behind/sides

**Blinding Mist** (迷雾):
- Blinds enemies for 5 seconds
- Can use Touch of Death on blinded targets
- Tier 3: +20% Touch of Death damage during effect

### Uses
- Stealth eliminations of guards/patrols
- Massive damage to unaware enemies
- Non-alert takedowns

## Endurance System

### Overview
**Endurance** is a resource separate from Health, Chi, Stamina, and Vitality

### What Endurance Does
- Affects enemy and player stamina
- Related to defensive actions
- Can be drained to create vulnerabilities

### Endurance Mechanics

**Endurance Recovery**:
- Characters naturally recover Endurance
- Can be reduced by skills

**Exhausted State**:
- Occurs when Endurance is depleted
- Mentioned in Serene Breeze Tier 4

### Skills Affecting Endurance

**Serene Breeze**:
- On hit: reduces target Endurance recovery by 50% for 8s
- Tier 4: Recovers 6 Qi if you're not Exhausted

**Ghostly Steps**:
- Tier 2: Reduces Endurance cost of ground dodge by 40%
- Tier 3: +10% Endurance reduction for all skills

**Talon Strike**:
- Tier 3: Reduces Endurance Recovery by 50% for 5s on defense break

## Shield System

### Shield Types

**HP Shield**:
- Temporary hit point buffer
- Can be granted by skills
- Absorbs damage before health

**Golden Body Shield**:
- 8-second duration
- Provides Fortitude and Damage Reduction while active
- Tier 2: Converts 50% of expired shield value to HP

### Skills Providing Shields

**Golden Body**:
- Grants Shield and Qi Recovery
- Tier 3: Can grant AoE shield to nearby allies (if Vitality ≥100)

**Free Morph**:
- Tier 3: +20% damage to targets with HP Shield

## Status Effect Immunity

### Mighty Enemies
"Mighty enemies" are immune to certain control effects

**Immune to**:
- Immobilization (Meridian Touch, Ghost Bind)
- Blinding (Blinding Mist)
- Other crowd control effects

**Not Immune to**:
- Damage
- Weak Point Breaks
- Debuffs (likely)

### Players
- Immune to Immobilization from Meridian Touch and Ghost Bind
- Immune to Blinding from Blinding Mist
