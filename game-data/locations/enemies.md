# Where Winds Meet - Enemies

## Enemy System Overview

Where Winds Meet features a diverse array of enemy types across its open world, ranging from human factions to wildlife and supernatural creatures.

**Enemy Categories**:
- **Human and Humanoid**: Various faction-affiliated enemies
- **Wildlife**: Aggressive animals like bears
- **Supernatural**: Magical creatures reflecting the Wuxia genre

**Rarity Classification**: Enemies are classified by rarity from regular grunts/mobs to faction bosses

**Pre-Release Note**: This information reflects the Early Access version. Enemy types, locations, and stats may change in the full release.

---

## Enemy Classification

### By Rarity

**Enemy Tiers** (from common to rare):
1. **Grunts/Mobs** - Common enemies in large numbers
2. **Elite Enemies** - Stronger variants with enhanced abilities
3. **Mini-Bosses** - Named enemies with unique mechanics
4. **Faction Bosses** - Powerful leaders requiring strategy and coordination

## Combat Strategy by Enemy Type

### Against Aureate Pavilion

**Threats**:
- Bell Bearers summon monkeys - priority target
- Gale Guardians have defensive abilities
- Commanders likely buff nearby troops

**Strategy**:
- Focus Bell Bearers first to prevent summons
- Use control abilities on Commanders
- Counter wind-based attacks from Gale Guardians

---

### Against Greenwood Wanderers

**Threats**:
- Horse Bandits are mobile and can disengage
- Swarm tactics with Pests
- Heisters likely have stealth/burst damage

**Strategy**:
- Use area attacks against Pest swarms
- Control abilities for Horse Bandits
- Stay alert for Heister ambushes

---

### Against Buddha Fort

**Threats**:
- Brute Monks have high damage
- Blindfolded Monks may have enhanced detection
- Sinbound Monks likely have corruption abilities

**Strategy**:
- Kite Brute Monks
- Stealth less effective against Blindfolded Monks
- Purification abilities useful against Sinbound Monks

---

### Against Song Dynasty Army

**Threats**:
- Musket Soldiers have dangerous ranged damage
- Shield Soldiers protect others
- Buglers call reinforcements

**Strategy**:
- Prioritize Buglers to prevent reinforcements
- Flank Shield Soldiers
- Stay mobile against Musket Soldiers
