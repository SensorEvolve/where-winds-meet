# Where Winds Meet - Factions

## Factions System Overview

Where Winds Meet features a complex web of political factions with intertwined storylines. These established organizations play crucial roles in the main story, and player actions can actively help or hinder them.

**Key Distinction - Factions vs Sects**:
- **Factions**: Established organizations integral to the main story
- **Sects**: Player-joinable groups for learning Martial Arts and special quests (don't affect main story)

**Information Access**: Faction details are compiled in the Journal under the World tab

**Pre-Release Note**: The game is not yet fully released, so this list is incomplete.

---

## Major Factions

### Northern Vow (Alliance)

**Type**: Heroic Resistance Organization

**Philosophy**: Bound solely by loyalty and righteousness

**Description**: Standing like a torch of hope in the lawless jianghu, the Northern Vow is a beacon for warriors who embrace ideals of honor and justice.

**Leadership**: Wang Qin

**Goals**: Retake the ancestral lands of Yanyun from invaders

---

#### History and Activities

**Major Campaign**: Zhongdu Bridge Campaign
- Fought to repel the Khitan invaders
- Heroes sacrificed their faces and futures
- Operated in shadowed underground palaces
- Guarded a grand and secret design

**Legacy**:
- Left behind significant relics after the campaign
- Created lasting impact on the region

---

#### Known Members

**Jiang Wulang (Uncle Jiang)**:
- Key member of the Northern Vow
- To be documented further

---

#### Territory and Influence

**Active Regions**:
- Yanman region
- Yanbei region

**Known Locations**:
- **Northern Vow Ruins** - Remnants of their operations
- **General's Shrine** - Memorial site from the Zhongdu Bridge Campaign

**Historical Significance**: Major force in northern territories before and during the Zhongdu Bridge Campaign

---

### Aureate Pavilion

**Type**: Infiltration and Subversion Organization

**Allegiance**: Jiangnan Kingdom

**Leadership**: Unknown (true identity kept secret)

**Reputation**: Ruthless cruelty and unscrupulous methods

---

#### Philosophy and Methods

**Member Profile**: Harbors those with misanthropic or apocalyptic desires

**Operating Style**:
- Ruthless cruelty
- Unscrupulous methods
- Infiltration and subversion
- Mass abduction and experimentation

---

#### Activities and Crimes

**Recent Operations in Qinghe**:
- **Abducted living souls** to forge Sleeping Puppets
- **Massacred outposts** throughout the region
- **Invaded Blissful Retreat** location
- Systematic infiltration of northern territories

**Motivation**: Appears to go beyond mere vengeance for the Breeze Post Incident

**Mystery**: True goals remain unclear but suggest larger conspiracy

---

#### Known Members

**Qianye (The Witch)**:
- Final boss of Chapter 1
- Major antagonist
- Represents Aureate Pavilion's threat

---

#### Territory and Influence

**Primary Operations**: Jiangnan Kingdom

**Expansion**: Gradually penetrated the entire Qinghe area in recent years

**Reach**: Extends influence across the jianghu to infiltrate both northern and southern territories

**Threat Level**: Major antagonistic force in the game

### Tiger Fort (Greenwood Bandits)

**Type**: Bandit Organization

**Leadership**: Three brothers - Zhang Loong, Zhang Hu, Zhang Bao

**Title**: Declared themselves "Greenwood Cheftain Du"

**Origin**: Rose when Palace of Annals fell silent and Aureate Pavilion stirred

---

#### Formation and Rise

**Founding Context**:
- Sensed opportunity in Qinghe's unrest
- Seized the deserted Twinbeast Ridge
- Founded Tiger Fort as their base
- Drew in drifters and outcasts

**Growth**: Quickly became a growing threat in the region

---

#### Leadership

**Zhang Loong**:
- Eldest brother
- Still holds Tiger Fort
- Recently suffered humiliating defeat

**Zhang Hu**:
- Second brother
- Still holds Tiger Fort with Zhang Loong
- Recently suffered humiliating defeat

**Zhang Bao**:
- Youngest brother
- Leads a crew northward
- Eyeing the Northern Vow Ruins
- Seeking legacy and lost treasures
- Recently suffered humiliating defeat

**Rumors**: All three brothers have recently been defeated by the same lone opponent (likely the player character)

---

#### Territory and Influence

**Base**: Tiger Fort (formerly Twinbeast Ridge)

**Operations**: Plague throughout the entire Qinghe region

**Activities**: Banditry, raiding, territorial expansion

**Threat**: Regional menace to travelers and settlements

---

### The Inkbound Order

**Type**: Philosophical/Governance Organization

**Origin**: Traces back to the Spring and Autumn Period (771-476 BCE)

**Philosophy**: Seeking the way of proper governance

**Status**: Neither officials nor commoners

---

#### History and Evolution

**Ancient Origins**: Spring and Autumn Period founding

**Consistency**: Through rises and falls, changing names but never purpose

**Core Mission**: Always sought the way of proper governance

**Longevity**: Survived dynasties and political upheavals while maintaining principles

---

#### Characteristics

**Martial Arts**: Rumors claim they possess no martial skills

**Presence**: Figures appear wherever jianghu storms brew

**Ideology**: Passionately upholding principles of saving people and bettering the world

**Approach**: Use influence and knowledge rather than martial prowess

---

#### Known Members

**Tang Xinci**:
- Part of the Gift of Gab Encounter Quest
- Representative of Inkbound Order philosophy
- Engages in dialogue-based missions

---

#### Territory and Influence

**Primary Activity**:
- Kaifeng
- Shandong region
- Other regions

**Dual Presence**:
- Disciples serving in imperial courts
- Disciples wandering the jianghu

**Influence**: Political and philosophical rather than military

**Goal**: Save people and better the world through proper governance

---

### Midnight Blades (Midnight Mercy)

**Type**: Extremist Religious/Philosophical Sect

**Alternative Name**: Midnight Mercy (same organization)

**Philosophy**: Ending suffering through death

---

#### Ideology

**Core Belief**: "The world is a burning house of endless suffering"

**Doctrine**:
- Forcibly prolonging lives only extends torment
- Letting the weak drown in suffering shows no true compassion
- Better to bear the karma of killing
- Ferry all beings to paradise
- Grant true peace and joy through death

**Justification**: View killing as mercy, releasing souls from suffering

---

#### Methods

**Approach**: Euthanasia-based philosophy taken to extremes

**Targets**: The weak, the suffering, those they deem better off dead

**Morality**: Willing to bear karma of killing for what they see as greater good

**Threat**: Dangerous extremism masked as compassion

---

#### Territory and Influence

**Original Activity**: War-torn northern regions

**Recent Expansion**: Began expanding southward with shifting tides of conflict

**Opportunity**: Exploits chaos and suffering of war zones

**Spread**: Growing influence as conflicts create more suffering

---

### Well of Heaven

**Type**: Charitable Martial Arts Sect

**Origin**: Snow mountains

**Reputation**: Wealth rivaling that of nations

**Reality**: All wealth scattered to aid the destitute

---

#### Philosophy

**Nature**: "Burns with a heart fiercer than flame" despite snowy origins

**Values**: Joy and purpose outweigh cold, lifeless treasures

**Practice**: Extreme philanthropy

**Attitude**: Rejection of material wealth for its own sake

---

#### Wealth and Charity

**Rumors**: Possess wealth rivaling that of nations

**Truth**: Known only to disciples - every coin has been scattered to aid the destitute across the land

**Approach**: Accumulate wealth only to give it away

**Impact**: Support the poor and suffering throughout the realm

---

#### Territory and Influence

**Headquarters**: Atop the northeastern snow mountains

**Activity**: Disciples active throughout the realm

**Reach**: Widespread charitable operations

**Connection**: Also appears as Jianghu Friend faction with disciples in various locations

---

## Faction Relationships

### Alliances

**Northern Vow**:
- Allied with resistance against Khitan invasion
- Opposed to foreign occupation

**Well of Heaven**:
- Charitable nature suggests cooperation with other heroic factions
- Likely allied with Northern Vow philosophically

---

### Antagonistic Relationships

**Aureate Pavilion vs Everyone**:
- Major antagonistic force
- Creating Sleeping Puppets (crime against humanity)
- Infiltrating all regions
- Primary villain faction

**Tiger Fort vs Law and Order**:
- Bandit faction
- Opposed by legitimate authorities
- Enemy to travelers and settlements

**Midnight Blades vs Civilization**:
- Extremist philosophy threatens everyone
- Expanding into new territories
- Dangerous to all they deem "suffering"

---

### Neutral/Complex

**The Inkbound Order**:
- Neither fully aligned with government nor jianghu
- Works with both imperial courts and wandering heroes
- Focus on governance and philosophy over factional conflict
