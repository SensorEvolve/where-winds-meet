# Where Winds Meet - Quests

> **Early Access Note**: This document reflects quest information from the Early Access version of Where Winds Meet. Quest details, rewards, and progression may change in future updates.

## Overview

Where Winds Meet features multiple quest categories including Main Story Campaigns, Career Quests, Lost Chapter Quests, and Exploration Quests. Each quest type contributes to character progression, world-building, and unlocking new content.

**Quest Approach**: Lost Chapter Quests can be followed or ignored in favor of the Main Quests, activities, or multiplayer elements. It is recommended that you mix up your approach, incorporating some story progression while also exploring the many facets of this title.

---

## Quest Categories

### Campaign Quests (Main Story)
**Description**: Primary storyline quests that advance the main narrative
**Characteristics**:
- Story-driven progression
- Unlocks new regions and features
- Major character development
- Campaign Challenge bosses upon completion

### Career Quests
**Description**: Profession-specific quest lines for Healer and Scholar careers
**Characteristics**:
- Career-exclusive content
- Unlocks career abilities and crafting recipes
- Professional skill progression

### Lost Chapter Quests
**Description**: Optional narrative quests that expand world lore and character stories
**Organization**: Organized by region (Qinghe Area, Kaifeng Area)
**Player Choice**: Can be followed or ignored in favor of Main Quests, activities, or multiplayer
**Characteristics**:
- Self-contained stories
- Multiple quest chains and volumes
- Rewards: Experience, items, lore revelations
- Recommended to mix with Main Quest progression for balanced experience

### Exploration Quests
**Description**: Discovery-based quests found while exploring the world
**Characteristics**:
- Found through exploration and NPC interactions
- Often tied to hidden locations
- May involve Butterflies guiding system

## Quest Integration with Other Systems

### Skill Theft Missions
Many Main Story and Side Story quests incorporate **Skill Theft** opportunities:
- Stealth missions to observe and learn Martial Arts
- Loong Keys collected during quests unlock new skills
- Campaign completion unlocks Campaign Challenge boss rematches

### Jianghu Friends (Old Friends)
Side Story quests often involve **Jianghu Friends NPCs**:
- Gift of Gab (AI Chat) quests build NPC reputation
- Completing quests raises reputation to Revered status
- Weekly gift rewards unlock after befriending NPCs

### Faction Relationships
Quest choices may affect **Faction Standing**:
- Northern Vow alliance quests
- Aureate Pavilion confrontations
- Tiger Fort interactions
- Inkbound Order missions
- Decisions impact faction relationships and story outcomes

### Exploration Integration
Quests guide players to discover:
- **Butterflies**: Following butterflies reveals quest objectives
- **Hidden Locations**: Secret areas unlocked through quest progression
- **Boundary Stones**: Vitality restore points marked during quests
- **Landmarks**: Points of interest revealed

### Crafting and Gathering
Quests reward and require:
- **Crafting Recipes**: Career Quests unlock exclusive recipes
- **Materials**: Quest rewards provide crafting materials
- **Gathering Locations**: Quests reveal resource nodes

## Quest Progression Tips

### For New Players
1. **Follow Main Story First**: Campaign unlocks core features and regions
2. **Complete Side Stories By Region**: Keep quest log organized
3. **Talk to All NPCs**: Many quests start from NPC conversations
4. **Follow Butterflies**: They often lead to hidden quests
5. **Check Quest Levels**: Some quests have recommended level ranges

### For Completionists
1. **Three-Volume Chains**: Complete all Kaifeng volume chains for full story
2. **Career Quests**: Don't miss profession-specific content
3. **Exploration Quests**: Thoroughly explore each region
4. **Faction Quests**: Engage with all major factions
5. **Gift of Gab Quests**: Use AI Chat system for unique outcomes

### For Efficient Progression
1. **Stack Regional Quests**: Complete multiple quests in same area
2. **Coordinate with Mount Skills**: Use Collection mounts while questing
3. **Plan Crafting Quests**: Gather materials during exploration quests
4. **Daily/Weekly Priority**: If dailies/weeklies exist, prioritize for rewards
5. **Campaign Challenges**: Replay story bosses for gear farming
