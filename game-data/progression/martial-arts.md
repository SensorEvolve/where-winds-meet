# Where Winds Meet - Martial Arts (武术)

## Martial Arts System Overview

**Total Count**: 12 Martial Arts

**Type**: Weapon-dependent combat skills

**Categories**: Offensive and Defensive abilities

**Acquisition**: Learned through progression, quests, or training

---

## How Martial Arts Work

### Weapon Dependency
- Martial Arts skills are tied to your equipped weapon
- Switch weapons = Switch available martial arts
- Each weapon type has unique martial arts

### Skill Categories

**Offensive Martial Arts**:
- Direct damage abilities
- Combo initiators and finishers
- Special attacks
- Charge attacks

**Defensive Martial Arts**:
- Blocks and parries
- Counter-attacks
- Evasive maneuvers
- Damage mitigation

## Martial Arts Progression

### Learning New Arts
- **Quest Rewards**: Special quests teach new techniques
- **Masters/Trainers**: NPC martial arts masters
- **Scrolls**: Find technique scrolls in the world
- **Level Unlocks**: Automatic learning at certain levels
- **Sect Membership**: Join martial arts sects for exclusive skills

### Skill Levels
- Martial Arts can likely be upgraded
- Higher levels = More damage/better effects
- May require practice or materials
