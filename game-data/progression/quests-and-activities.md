## Quest System Overview

**Main Story**: 100-150+ hours of narrative content

## Quest Types

### 1. Main Story Quests

**Purpose**: Drive the narrative forward

**Characteristics**:
- Linear progression through the story
- Uncover character's identity
- Experience the Five Dynasties era
- Unlock new regions
- Required for progression

**Rewards**:
- Experience
- Story progression
- Key items
- New abilities
- Region unlocks

### 3. World Quests / Dynamic Events

**Purpose**: Open world activities

**Characteristics** (Assumed):
- Appear in world dynamically
- Group participation possible

### 4. Martial Arts Quests

**Purpose**: Learn new techniques

**Characteristics**:
- Train with masters
- Complete challenges

- Unlock martial arts

**Rewards**:

- Mystic skills
- Technique upgrades

### 5. Daily/Weekly Quests

**Purpose**: Regular engagement and rewards

### 6. Career Quests

**Rewards**:
